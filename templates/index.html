What is Linkstr?


Linkstr is a link shortening service aimed at professionals and businesses that want to turn links they share into an additional channel of advertising. Turn clicks on content into views of a specific, targeted advertisement campaign or show 3rd party advertisements in order to directly generate revenue.

Interstitial Pages


Linkstr allows you to include interstitial pages when users click on links you have shortened. The content of these pages can be anything from a sale to a third party advertisement that allows you to directly generate revenue. See an example here.

In Depth Analytics


With our web dashboard and upcoming native mobile applications can gather insight into where your users are, what kind of browsers they use, and where they are coming from. Built on state-of-the-art HTML5 technologies, Linkstr's responsive design works on even the smallest of screen sizes.
