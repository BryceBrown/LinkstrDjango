About Us

Since the dawn of facebook, social media marketing has been one of the strongest ways to advertise a product, service, or local business. It allows organizations that just 10 years ago would have struggled to manage relationships with their customers to keep an always open point of contact. Sites like Twitter have allowed the spread of events and promotions with a speed and accurracy of never before. Linkstr's goal is to make this channel of communication as effective as possible, by gathering precise, calculable metrics and displaying them in an actionble format.

Linkstr's main service is providing link shortening and analytics links that you share with your users. This information can be used in what audience that you target when launching marketing campaigns.

What is Linkstr?

Linkstr is a brand new link shortening service aimed at professionals and businesses that want to turn links they use into an additional channel of advertising. Turn clicks on content into views of a specific, targeted advertisement campaign or show 3rd party advertisements in order to directly generate revenue.


In Depth Analytics

With our web dashboard and upcoming native mobile applications can gather insight into where your users are, what kind of browsers they use, and where they are coming from. Built on state-of-the-art HTML5 technologies, Linkstr's responsive design works on even the smallest of screen sizes.


You Own Your Data

With Linkstr, you are able to export all data generated through your use of our application to a CSV file, allowing for personal backup and analysis. Your profile is private, and no one but you can see information about the links you have generated. We also have a variety of reports that can be generated based off of the data that you have collected.
